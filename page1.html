WELCOME TO PS3M0DIFI3D

What you’ll need:

• Playstation Console on Firmware 3.55 or Lower (Check Page (Other Methods) for more details)

• USB Flashdrive, 8GB Minimum (Add troubleshooting page for SD method)

• Internet connection to download recourses & Games later

What to do:

Step 1: Download FW

Step 2: Format USB to Fat32, Make Folders Called PS3>PS3Data Paste Fw here

Step 3: On your XMB, Scroll to System Update > From USB storage > Install

Step 4: Wait…..

Step 5: Done, Now Check if you are on the correct version.
